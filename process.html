Artisanship

How We Create

A journey from a single thread to a masterpiece. Every garment takes 30+ hours of dedicated handwork.

01

Consultation & Fabric

Your journey begins with a coffee and a conversation. We guide you through our library of over 5,000 fabrics—from Italian Loro Piana wools to fine Thai Silks. We discuss your lifestyle, the climate you live in, and the purpose of the suit.

  • Style advice for your body type
  • Button & lining customization
  • Initial measurements (35+ points)
02

The First Fitting

Within 24-48 hours, we prepare a "basted" garment. This is a temporary version of your suit held together by white basting thread. This allows us to adjust the drape and flow before the final fabric is cut permanently.

03

Hand-Finishing

Our master tailors then spend hours on the fine details: hand-rolled lapels, functional "surgeon" cuffs, and silk-thread buttonholes. Every seam is pressed using traditional heavy irons to "lock" the shape into the fibers.

04

Final Delivery

After a final quality check, your garment is ready. We offer complimentary delivery to your hotel in Pattaya or worldwide express shipping to your home address.

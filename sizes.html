Measurement Guide

For the perfect fit, we require detailed body measurements. Use this guide as a reference.

Men's Essentials

Area How to Measure
Neck Around the base of the neck where the collar sits.
Chest Widest part of the chest, under the armpits.
Shoulder From the edge of one shoulder bone to the other.
Sleeve From shoulder seam to the wrist bone.
Waist Natural waistline (usually near the navel).
Inseam From the crotch down to the desired pant length.

Women's Essentials

Area How to Measure
Bust Fullest part of the bust, keeping tape level.
Waist Smallest part of the torso (natural waist).
Hips Fullest part of the hips and buttocks.
Dress Length From the high point of the shoulder to the hem.
Shoulder Width Across the back, from shoulder tip to tip.

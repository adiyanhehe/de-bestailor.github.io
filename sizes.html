Precision Fit

Measurement Guide

To ensure a flawless silhouette, please provide the following measurements or visit us for a professional fitting.

Men's Essentials

Area How to Measure
NeckAround the base of the neck where the collar sits.
ChestWidest part of the chest, under the armpits.
ShoulderFrom the edge of one shoulder bone to the other.
SleeveFrom shoulder seam to the wrist bone.
WaistNatural waistline (usually near the navel).
InseamFrom the crotch down to the desired pant length.

Women's Essentials

Area How to Measure
BustFullest part of the bust, keeping tape level.
WaistSmallest part of the torso (natural waist).
HipsFullest part of the hips and buttocks.
Dress LengthFrom the high point of the shoulder to the hem.
Shoulder WidthAcross the back, from shoulder tip to tip.

The Bespoke Standard

Measurement Guide

Precise data is the foundation of a masterpiece. Follow our professional guide.

01

Neck

Measure around the base of the neck where the collar sits. Add 1cm for a comfortable fit.

02

Chest

Wrap the tape under your armpits around the fullest part of your chest. Keep the tape level.

03

Shoulder Width

Measure from the edge of the left shoulder bone across the back to the right shoulder bone.

04

Sleeve Length

From the shoulder bone down to your wrist bone with your arm relaxed.

05

The Waist

Measure around your natural waistline (at the navel). Stand naturally.

06

Jacket Length

From the base of the neck down to your desired length (usually center of palm).

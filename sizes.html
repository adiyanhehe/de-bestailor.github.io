The Master Fit

Measurement Guide

Precise measurements are the foundation of bespoke tailoring. Follow our professional guide below for a flawless silhouette.

01

The Neck

Measure around the base of the neck where the collar sits. Add 1cm for a comfortable "two-finger" fit.

02

The Chest

Wrap the tape under your armpits around the fullest part of your chest. Keep the tape level and breathe normally.

03

Shoulder Width

Measure from the edge of the left shoulder bone, across the back to the edge of the right shoulder bone.

04

Sleeve Length

From the shoulder bone down to your wrist bone. Keep your arm relaxed at your side.

05

The Waist

Measure around your natural waistline (at the navel). Do not suck in your stomach.

06

Jacket Length

From the base of the neck down to your desired length (usually center of the palm).
